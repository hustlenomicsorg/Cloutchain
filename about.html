About CloutChain

CloutChain is a platform built to give power back to creators. Our mission is simple: make brand deals fair, transparent, and accessible for creators of all sizes.

Our Mission

We believe creators should be rewarded fairly for their influence. Too often, creators are underpaid or overlooked. CloutChain bridges the gap between brands and creators with trust and simplicity.

Our Vision

We see a future where creators of every scale — from micro-influencers to big names — can unlock sustainable income streams without middlemen exploiting them.

What We Do

At CloutChain, we provide creators with:

  • ✔️ Transparent brand collaborations
  • ✔️ Fair payment models
  • ✔️ Equal opportunities for micro-creators
