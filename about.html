About CloutChain

Our mission is to revolutionize creator-brand collaborations by making deals fair, transparent, and accessible for everyone.

Our Mission

We believe creators deserve to be paid fairly for their influence. CloutChain bridges the gap between brands and creators through trust, transparency, and simplicity.

Our Vision

We envision a future where micro-influencers and top creators alike have equal access to opportunities, without exploitation by middlemen or unfair contracts.

What We Do

We provide creators with tools to connect directly with brands, negotiate fairly, and unlock premium opportunities. Our platform ensures payments are secure and collaborations are smooth.
