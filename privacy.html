Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 2025

1. Overview

CloutChain ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, who we share it with, and your choices about your information. By using CloutChain you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information we collect

  • Account & contact data: name, email, display name, profile links (YouTube/Instagram handle), profile image.
  • Creator metrics: follower/subscriber counts, engagement metrics, channel/page URLs and public statistics you provide or link.
  • Payment & billing data: payment tokens or identifiers required to process purchases and refunds. (We do not store full card numbers; payments handled by secure gateways.)
  • Usage data: IP address, device type, pages visited, and analytics data for product improvement.
  • Communications: messages exchanged with support or brands, email preferences.

3. How we use your information

  • To operate and provide the services (match creators with brands and manage onboarding).
  • To process payments, issue refunds, and prevent fraud.
  • To communicate important updates, transactional messages, and support responses.
  • To analyze and improve product features and platform performance.
  • To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests from authorities.

4. Sharing and disclosures

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited information with third parties only as necessary:

  • Brands: selected creator metrics (followers, engagement) and profile links — only to evaluate fit for a campaign.
  • Service providers: payment processors and analytics providers who process information under our instruction.
  • Legal: when required by law or to protect rights and safety.

5. Cookies & analytics

We use cookies and analytics tools to understand site usage and improve the product. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not function correctly without them.

6. Security & retention

We use industry-standard measures to protect data (encryption in transit, limited access). We retain data only as long as necessary to provide services, meet legal requirements, or as you request deletion.

7. Your rights

  • Access: You can request a copy of your personal data we hold.
  • Correction: You may request updates to inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: You may request account deletion; certain data may be retained for legal/operational reasons.
  • Opt-out: You may opt-out of marketing emails; transactional emails necessary to operate your account will still be sent.

8. Children

CloutChain is not intended for users under 13. If you are under the age required by your jurisdiction, please do not register.

9. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on the site with an updated effective date.

10. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us at support@cloutchain.example.
