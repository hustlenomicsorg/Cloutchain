Onboarding Guidelines

To maintain professionalism and ensure high-quality creator experiences, CloutChain does not allow onboarding of YouTubers with fewer than 3,000 subscribers unless the channel meets exceptional quality standards.

Channels must demonstrate consistent content, community engagement, and originality. This helps us guarantee fair opportunities for all creators and maintain platform credibility.
